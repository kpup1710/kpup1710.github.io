About

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Mathematics at the National University of Singapore under the co-supervision of Prof. Tan Nguyen and Prof. Zhang Lei. My interests lie in mathematics and its applications, particularly in optimization and deep learning. I aspire to an academic career, contributing to both the theoretical and practical aspects of mathematics in AI.

My research focuses on uncovering the mathematical intuition behind AI models and leveraging mathematical tools to optimize models and enhance computational efficiency. I seek to collaborate with researchers who share my enthusiasm for advancing mathematical knowledge and developing innovative solutions to complex problems.

Research Approach Levels
